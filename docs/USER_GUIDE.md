Welcome to IntraVox! This guide helps you navigate and use your organization's intranet.

## Getting Started

### Accessing IntraVox

1. Log in to Nextcloud
2. Click the IntraVox icon in the top menu bar
3. You'll see the homepage of your intranet

## Navigation

### Main Menu

The main navigation appears at the top of every page:

- **Menu items**: Click to navigate to different sections
- **Dropdowns**: Hover or click items with arrows to see submenus
- **Megamenu**: Some intranets use a large dropdown showing all options

![Megamenu Navigation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nextcloud/intravox/main/screenshots/megamenu.png)

*Megamenu showing all navigation options at a glance*

### Breadcrumbs

Below the navigation, breadcrumbs show your current location:

```
Home > Departments > HR > Policies
```

Click any breadcrumb to go back to that level.

### Sidebar Navigation

Some pages have a sidebar for quick access to related content.

## Language Selection

If your intranet supports multiple languages:

1. Look for the language selector (usually top-right)
2. Click your preferred language
3. The page content switches to that language

Available languages depend on your organization's setup.

## Reading Content

### Page Layout

Pages can have different layouts:

- **Single column**: Content spans the full width
- **Two columns**: Content side by side
- **Three columns**: Multiple content areas

### Content Types

You'll encounter various content types:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Headings | Section titles (different sizes) |
| Text | Paragraphs, lists, formatted text |
| Images | Photos, diagrams, screenshots (may be clickable to navigate to other pages) |
| Videos | Embedded videos from YouTube, Vimeo, PeerTube, or local uploads |
| Links | Quick links to other pages or external sites |
| Dividers | Visual separators between sections |

### Images

- Click images to view them larger (if enabled)
- Some images may be clickable and navigate to other pages or external websites
- Clickable images show a cursor change when hovering
- Images may have captions describing their content

### Videos

- Click the play button to start a video
- Videos may autoplay (muted) depending on settings
- Use video controls to pause, seek, or adjust volume
- Some videos open in fullscreen mode when clicked

### Links

Links appear in different styles:

- **Text links**: Underlined text, click to navigate
- **Link cards**: Boxes with title, description, and icon
- **External links**: Open in a new tab (indicated by icon)

## Search

### Using Search

1. Click the search icon (üîç) in the navigation
2. Type your search terms
3. Press Enter or click Search
4. Browse the results

### Search Tips

- Use specific keywords for better results
- Search looks in page titles and content
- Results show relevance scores

## Page Information

### Page Details

Some pages show metadata:

- **Title**: The page name
- **Last modified**: When the page was last updated
- **Modified by**: Who made the last change

### Side Panel

Click the info icon to see:

- Page properties
- Modification history
- Related pages

## Common Tasks

### Finding Information

1. **Use navigation**: Browse through menu structure
2. **Use search**: Search for specific topics
3. **Use breadcrumbs**: Navigate back to parent sections

### Returning to Homepage

- Click the logo in the top-left corner
- Or click "Home" in the navigation

### Printing a Page

1. Navigate to the page you want to print
2. Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P)
3. Pages are optimized for printing

### Sharing a Page

1. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar
2. Share the link with colleagues
3. They'll see the page (if they have access)

## Mobile Usage

IntraVox works on mobile devices:

## Accessibility

IntraVox supports accessibility features:

- Keyboard navigation (Tab, Enter, Escape)
- Screen reader compatible
- High contrast with Nextcloud dark mode
- Scalable text (use browser zoom)

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Tab | Move to next interactive element |
| Enter | Activate buttons and links |
| Escape | Close menus and dialogs |
| Arrow keys | Navigate within menus |

## Troubleshooting

### Page Not Loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact IT support if the problem persists

### Access Denied

If you see "Access Denied" or can't view a page:

1. You may not have permission to view this content
2. Contact your manager or IT to request access
3. Some content is restricted to specific departments

### Images Not Showing

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Images may take time to load on slow connections

### Search Not Working

1. Try different keywords
2. Check spelling
3. Use broader search terms

## Getting Help

### Contact Options

- **IT Support**: For technical issues
- **Content questions**: Contact the page owner (see page details)
- **General inquiries**: Use the Contact page in IntraVox

### Feedback

If you have suggestions for improving content:

1. Contact the content editor for that section
2. Use your organization's feedback process
3. Speak with your department's intranet representative
